Privacy Policy for ToolzzHub.com

Last Updated: September 08, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ToolzzHub.com ("we," "us," or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our website, ToolzzHub.com (the "Site").

2. Information We Collect

We have designed our Site to minimize the collection of personal information.

Information You Provide Voluntarily

The only personal information we collect is what you voluntarily provide to us when you contact us via our email address (connect@toolzzhub.com). This includes your email address and any other information you choose to provide in your correspondence. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiries.

Information Collected Automatically

Third-Party Services (Google AdSense): We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Site. Google and its partners may collect data automatically to provide personalized ads. This data can include cookies, device identifiers, IP addresses, and browsing behavior. ToolzzHub.com does not have access to, control over, or store any of the information collected by Google.

Server Logs: Like most websites, our web server may automatically log standard access information, such as your IP address, browser type, and access times. This information is used for site administration and technical troubleshooting and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

3. Use of Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

Our partner, Google AdSense, uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our Site or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our Site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You can learn how Google collects and uses data by visiting: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

You can also control or disable cookies through your individual browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this and other websites.

4. Your Data Protection Rights

As we do not directly collect or control the data used for advertising, you should exercise your rights directly with the relevant third parties, such as Google. Your rights may include:

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU/UK Residents: You have rights including access, rectification, erasure, and the right to object to processing. Consent for data collection for advertising is typically managed through a cookie consent banner on our Site.

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA): You have the right to know what personal information is being collected and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information. To exercise this right regarding advertising data, please visit Google's Ads Settings. We do not sell any personal information we may hold (such as from email correspondence).

Canada (PIPEDA): We comply with Canadian privacy laws. Consent for the collection of data by our third-party partners is obtained through industry-standard mechanisms.

6. Data Security

7. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly remove it.
